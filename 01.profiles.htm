Brockett Horne

Chair

Brockett Horne is a graphic designer who first learned of letterforms when examining billboards from the backseat of her parents station wagon along I-95 just outside of her hometown.As a professional in Chicago, she created exhibit spaces and collateral for museums. Her creative work is inspired by a desire to encourage the spectator to look intensely. She holds a B.F.A. from Carnegie Mellon University, an M.F.A. from Rhode Island School of Design (with double thesae in art history and graphic design), and is an active member of the AIGA, SEGD, and CAA.
